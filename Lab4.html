PUDGELING COURIER

Pudgling is given out to all owners of The International Compendium 2014. Players with a compendium of over level 50 are also granted the Pudgling Golden Hook Upgrade tool item which unlocks a secondary style.

It's 150 pesos in the steam market and currently increasing in price


GOLDEN BABY ROSHAN

This courier was given out during the Diretide 2012 event to players who made it into the Sugar Rush Hall of Fame, by setting the record for killing Roshan the fastest. It shares a model with Baby Roshan, except with golden textures and effects.

This courier is for 1000 pesos in the steam market and currently increasing it's price


ENDURING WAR DOG

The war dog is a type of courier - an in-game animal which ferries purchases from the item shops in the Dota 2 arena to the players, to avoid a player having to leave the front lines.

This courier is for 38000 dollars in steam market
